# Product Overview

## Purpose

Project Osprey is a local-first iOS voice-capture utility for field contractors. It validates the voice-first field inspection concept by providing an eyes-free, high-durability interface to capture photos paired with voice memos.

## Target Users

- Field contractors performing inspections
- Users who need hands-free/eyes-free operation
- Mobile workers in environments where screen interaction is impractical

## Key Features

- Single-tap photo capture with automatic voice memo recording
- Haptic feedback for state transitions (eyes-free operation)
- Chronological gallery feed of inspection items
- Fully offline — no backend, no API, no cloud storage

## Business Objectives

- Validate the voice-first field inspection concept as an MVP
- Minimize friction in the capture workflow (one tap to start)
- Ensure high durability and reliability for field conditions
